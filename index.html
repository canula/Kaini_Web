Tone Calculator

Create inspiring melodies in seconds.

The TC-01 Tone Calculator turns your ideas into melodies easily. Hum a tune, flip an existing melody, or generate something new — then export to your DAW. Available for download on all devices.

PWA App (Cross Platform)

Features

Record Voice

Hum a melody or import and audio file and TC-01 transcribes it into notes instantly.

MIDI Import

Bring in MIDI files from your DAW or other apps. Edit, transform, and build on melodies you've already started. Save the rhythm, contour, or melodic motion of proven melodies and apply them to others to create entirely new compositions.

Motion / Contour / Rhythm Presets

Choose from built-in motion shapes, contour curves, and rhythm patterns. Mix and match presets to discover unexpected melodies.

Phrase Generation

Generate complete musical phrases with one tap. TC-01 combines your settings into playable melodies — tweak the results or start fresh.

Microtonal Support

Go beyond standard tuning. TC-01 include microtonal scales, allowing you to expand the sonic palette of your melodies, into new and exciting territories.

Note Edit Tracker

See every change you make, note by note. The edit tracker keeps a full history so you can undo, compare, and refine your melodies.
