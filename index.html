Features

Everything you need to create melodies your way.

Record Voice

Hum a melody and TC-01 transcribes it into notes instantly. No keyboard, no theory — just sing what's in your head.

MIDI Import

Bring in MIDI files from your DAW or other apps. Edit, transform, and build on melodies you've already started.

Motion / Contour / Rhythm Presets

Choose from built-in motion shapes, contour curves, and rhythm patterns. Mix and match presets to discover unexpected melodies.

Phrase Generation

Generate complete musical phrases with one tap. TC-01 combines your settings into playable melodies — tweak the results or start fresh.

Microtonal Support

Go beyond standard tuning. TC-01 supports microtonal scales so you can explore quarter-tones, just intonation, and custom tunings.

Note Edit Tracker

See every change you make, note by note. The edit tracker keeps a full history so you can undo, compare, and refine your melodies.
